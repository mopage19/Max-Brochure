                                    Lochdock Leg Kits • Maxlocs • Maxstands12 For More Options & Accessories Visit: www.maxdock.comThe Maxloc patented cross-arm system has an integral CNC machined clamping system that contacts the entire dock pole. A 2” x 4” extrusion forms the main body, and machined stringer brackets bolt into the clamp system, providing an extremely secure, stable connection point for the dock sections to attach. Unlike the traditional cross arms clamps and optional stringer brackets that fail at a high rate. The Maxloc base is a machined 2’ x 4” extrusion that wont sag or slip under heavy foot traffic, and easily out performs lesser supports.MaxStand stanchions were developed to provide a strong platform for Max Standard & Signature dock. The stands main extrusion is the proprietary MSE or MaxStand extrusion, this extrusion was originally intended to be just for MaxStands but you now can see it in other Max products for its strength and versatility. The stands MSE horizontal support beam legs are machined at a 7° Angle to give a wider stance at the bottom with welded foot pads standard. The wider platform stands 9’ to 12’ and up come with center legs kits that are bolted into the MSE. This creates one of the strongest stand alone dock supports on the market today. The adjustability of the stand makes it the number one replacement stand for older docks in the field.Lochdock Leg Kits are mounted internally with inside the Lochdock referred to as LD2 Or Lochdock Plus PL frames. Some kits can be used on both docks and some are specific to each. If you read the descriptions you can see which ones those are, your Max dealer can also help you in making sure you have the correct ones for the dock layout you are making. These components that make up the leg kits are going to provide two directional bracing for your system making it extremely strong & stable. MaxStands MaxStands 4 Ft. to 16 Ft.
